* Below rates are exclusive of GST

Description:

Biharinath Ecotourism centre is located near Biharinath Temple and is at the foot hills of Biharinath Hills. Biharinath Hill is the tallest Hill in Bankura District with an altitude of 451mts. Biharinath is the other name of lord Mahadev or Shiva. The famous Biharinath Temple is located just in front of the ecotourism Centre. Huge fairs are organised during Shivaratri and saraswati Puja. Biharinath is also well know trekking point and trekking starts from eco cottages only. The centre has 4 Cottages.
