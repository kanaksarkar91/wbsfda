Eco Tourism Destinations

Beharinath ETC

Bihari, PO: Bamuntore, Bankura Beharinath ETC Dist: Bankura Pin code: 722101

Description:

Biharinath Ecotourism centre is located near Biharinath Temple and is at the foot hills of Biharinath Hills. Biharinath Hill is the tallest Hill in Bankura District with an altitude of 451mts. Biharinath is the other name of lord Mahadev or Shiva. 

Price starts from

₹300.00 per night

Timing:

Check in: 12:00 PM Check out: 11:00 AM

Parimal Kanan

Chandrakona Road Urban Recreation Forestry ETC Dist: Pachim Medinipur Pin code: 745230

It is located about half km from Chandrakona Road railway station. The park is spread over 30 ha with small forests. There is rose garden, flower garden, Aviary, medicinal plants garden, arboretum, boating, Aquarium,children garden, toy train and picnic sheds.

₹300.00 per night
